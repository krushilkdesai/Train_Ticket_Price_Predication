About RailFare

RailFare is an intelligent train ticket price prediction platform powered by a Random Forest regression model trained on real Indian Railways fare data. Our system analyzes train type, source city, and destination to deliver accurate fare estimates in real time.

Whether you're planning a business trip on the Rajdhani Express or a budget journey on Garib Rath, RailFare helps you make informed travel decisions with confidence.
